# Drive MVP — Support

Need a hand with **Drive MVP**? We're a small team that ships updates fast, and we're glad to help.

## Get in touch

Email us at **[support@ice-24.com](mailto:support@ice-24.com)** — we typically reply within **1–2 business days**.

To get you sorted faster, it helps to include:

- Your device and OS (e.g. iPhone 15 / iOS 18, or Samsung Galaxy S24 / Android 14)
- The app version
- A short note on what happened and what you expected

## Quick answers

- **Trips aren't recording.** Set the app's location permission to **Always** and make sure background / battery optimization isn't restricting Drive MVP — trip detection runs in the background.
- **Sign-in or account trouble.** Email us from the address tied to your account and we'll help you get back in.
- **Privacy requests** (access, correction, deletion). See the **[Privacy Policy](privacy.html)**, or just email us and we'll take care of it.
