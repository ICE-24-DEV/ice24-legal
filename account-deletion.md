# Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your **Drive MVP** account and the data associated with it at any time.

## How to request deletion

**Email [support@ice-24.com](mailto:support@ice-24.com)** with the subject **"Delete my account"**, from the email address tied to your account.

You can also delete your account in the app: **Profile → Settings → Delete my account**.

## What gets deleted

- Your account and profile (name, contact details, vehicle and driver info)
- Your recorded trips and driving-behavior scores
- Delivery and condition photos you captured
- Telematics data held on your behalf by our processor, **DriveQuant** — we trigger its deletion as part of your request

Deletion takes effect **within 30 days**.

## What we may keep

We may retain limited records where the law requires it (e.g. tax or audit obligations), as described in our [Privacy Policy](privacy.html). Anything retained is anonymized or aggregated and is no longer tied to your identity.

## Questions

Email **[support@ice-24.com](mailto:support@ice-24.com)** — we typically reply within 1–2 business days.
